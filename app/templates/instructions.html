How to Work with the AI Assistant

1

Get Initial Recommendation

For each programming question, you must first click the "Get Initial Recommendation" button to receive the AI's initial suggestion.

2

Ask Follow-up Questions

After receiving the initial recommendation, you can engage in a conversation with the AI:

  • Ask for explanations
  • Request clarifications
  • Challenge its responses
  • Seek additional information

3

Make Your Decision

While the AI is here to assist you, the final answer choice is yours. Take your time to understand both the question and the AI's explanation.

4

Complete the Follow-up Survey

After each programming question, you'll answer a brief survey about your interaction with the AI.

Important Notes

  • All interactions with the AI are recorded for research purposes
  • There are no right or wrong ways to interact with the AI - we want to observe your natural interaction
  • Take as much time as you need with each question
  • You'll complete a short survey after each programming question
  • The AI is here to assist, but the final decision is always yours
